About Urban Redevelopment Consultants

Premium redevelopment consultancy delivering institutional-grade advisory services to housing societies.

Who We Are

A Partnership Built on Expertise

Urban Redevelopment Consultants is a premium redevelopment consultancy firm established by a senior Structural Engineer and a practicing Architect, created to deliver institutional-grade advisory services to housing societies and asset owners.

Our practice is founded on the belief that redevelopment is not merely construction—it is a high-value transformation of a collective asset. By integrating structural engineering rigor, architectural intelligence, regulatory insight, and financial feasibility, we deliver redevelopment strategies that are secure, optimized, and future-ready.

We operate exclusively as a society-appointed, independent consultant, ensuring unbiased guidance, disciplined decision-making, and long-term value creation.

Er. Rupesh Kale

Structural Engineer & Redevelopment Consultant ME Civil | FIE | Chartered Engineer (India)

Mr. Rupesh Kale is a Chartered Engineer (India) with over 23+ years of professional experience, including 15 years of hands-on industry experience with leading organizations and 8 years as an independent practicing consulting engineer.

He holds a master's degree in civil engineering from the University of Mumbai and is a Registered Structural Engineer with the Government of Maharashtra, MCGM, NMMC, TMC, and PMC, and a Licensed Surveyor with MCGM. He is also a Fellow Member of The Institution of Engineers (India) [FIE] and a Member of the Indian Society of Structural Engineers (ISSE).

During his corporate tenure, he was associated with renowned organizations such as L&T Realty, Lodha Group, Oberoi Realty, Reliance Energy Ltd. (ADA Group), and Man Realty, gaining in-depth experience in the execution of high-rise buildings of complex structural nature and high-end finishes.

As an independent consulting engineer, Mr. Kale has successfully led and delivered assignments involving structural audits, major repairs, building rehabilitation, and Project Management Consultancy (PMC) for redevelopment projects, working closely with housing societies, developers, and statutory authorities.

Service 02

Structural Audit & Technical Due Diligence

A thorough, statutorily compliant assessment of your building's structural health, providing the foundation for repair or redevelopment decisions.

  • Detailed structural audit of RCC and load-bearing structures
  • Visual inspection and condition mapping
  • Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) coordination and interpretation
  • Assessment of residual life and safety grading
  • Repair vs redevelopment recommendations
  • Statutory-compliant audit reporting for society decision-making

Our Expertise Spans

Redevelopment Models We Handle

We provide tailored advisory across all prevalent redevelopment structures, ensuring the best fit for your society.

Builder-Led Redevelopment

Traditional model where a developer undertakes the entire project on behalf of the society.

Society Self-Redevelopment

Society manages the project directly, retaining full control over costs, quality, and timelines.

Development Manager Model

A professional manager handles execution while the society retains ownership and decision authority.

Joint Redevelopment

Collaborative model with shared responsibilities between the society and development partner.

Cluster Redevelopment

Large-scale redevelopment involving multiple adjacent societies or buildings as a unified project.
